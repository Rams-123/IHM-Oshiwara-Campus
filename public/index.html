    Why study in ITM Oshiwara?

    The ITM Oshiwara campus is located amidst the residential buildings in one of Mumbai’s upscale localities. It is a modern building, upgraded with state-of-the-art infrastructure to deliver world-class education and hands-on learning opportunities

    ITM Oshiwara is committed to educating students in hotel management using cutting-edge teaching strategies, learning methodologies, and a research-based curriculum to prepare them for careers worldwide.

    Key Highlights

    • International exposure for experienced teachers
    • Numerous fascinating activities, including study tours, industry visits, stipend-paid internships, and more
    • Curriculum innovation
    • Interaction with eminent hoteliers, famous chefs, and industry professionals
    • 100% guaranteed placement assistance
    • A user-friendly industry interface
    • Separate kitchens, from basic, to advance, for specialised learning
    • Separate IT labs for students to conduct research
    • Front-office lab, mock guest rooms, and advanced training restaurants for practical learning

    Why Hospitality Industry?

    As one of India’s biggest employment sectors, the Hospitality industry is predicted to generate 53 Million job opportunities by the year 2029! As a customer-facing industry, the specialized sector needs special skills to cater to multiple customer touch-points. The evolving customers need their experience to be the best, smooth and nurtured. ITM IHM is one of the best institutes for hotel management in Mumbai & Navi Mumbai with the biggest and largest infrastructure for hotel management education.

    Soft Skill Training by Corporate Trainers:

    • The “TALEWIND” department covers all the soft skills essential to bridge the gap between campus to an industry.
    • The course curriculum ranges from verbal & written communication , public speaking, assertiveness, empathy,presentation skills, life skills enhancement and career management.
    • The faculty members are certified and equipped with soft skills; assured focused attention, conduct remedial sessions as well as mentor every student for their internship and final placement.
    • Students are well trained in presentation skills, etiquette, grooming, body language, group discussion & personal interview.
    • TALEWIND is a holistic program meant to transform & equip students for the hospitality industry and bag quality job placements.
